Sustainable Packaging through ESG Practices
We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint by
adopting ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) principles in
our packaging for online product shipments.
•  Eco-friendly materials: We use recyclable and biodegradable
packaging to minimize waste.
•  Customer awareness: We encourage our customers to recycle and
reuse packaging, fostering a culture of sustainability.
Renewable Energy – Solar Panel Installation
To further strengthen our sustainability efforts, we have invested in
solar energy infrastructure within our company premises.
•  Clean energy generation: Our solar panels provide a significant
portion of our electricity needs, reducing reliance on non-renewable
sources.
•  Carbon footprint reduction: By harnessing renewable energy, we
cut down greenhouse gas emissions and contribute to climate change
mitigation.
•  Long-term commitment: This initiative reflects our dedication to
building a greener future and aligning with Malaysia’s renewable
energy goals.
